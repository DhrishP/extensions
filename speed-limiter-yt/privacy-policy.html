Privacy Policy for YouTube Speed Limiter

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

YouTube Speed Limiter is a browser extension that allows users to set a maximum playback speed for YouTube videos. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The extension operates entirely within your browser and does not communicate with any external servers.

Data Storage

The extension stores your speed limit preference locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is not shared with us or any third parties
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the extension

How It Works

The extension:

  • Runs only on YouTube.com pages
  • Monitors video playback speed
  • Automatically reduces speed when it exceeds your limit
  • Does not track your viewing habits or video preferences

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. The extension does not integrate with external APIs or services.

Data Security

Since no data leaves your device, there are no external security risks. Your speed limit preference is stored securely in Chrome's extension storage system.

Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this extension.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any data stored by the extension
  • Delete your stored preferences by uninstalling the extension
  • Opt out of using the extension entirely

This extension is designed with privacy in mind and operates with minimal data collection to provide a simple, effective speed limiting experience.
